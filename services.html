Our Services

At Prestige Pressure Washing, we offer comprehensive pressure washing services for residential, commercial, and marine applications. Our experienced team uses professional-grade equipment and environmentally friendly cleaning solutions to deliver exceptional results for every project.

Keep your home looking its best with our comprehensive residential pressure washing services. We help homeowners throughout the Stockton area restore and maintain the beauty of their properties.

Driveway & Walkway Cleaning

Remove oil stains, dirt, mold, and mildew from concrete, pavers, and other surfaces to restore your driveway and walkways to like-new condition.

House Siding Washing

Safely remove dirt, algae, and mildew from vinyl, wood, brick, stucco, and other siding materials without causing damage.

Deck & Patio Cleaning

Restore the beauty of your outdoor living spaces by removing dirt, mold, and mildew from decks, patios, and outdoor furniture.

Fence Cleaning

Roof Cleaning

Safely remove moss, algae, and debris from your roof to prevent damage and maintain your home's curb appeal.

Gutter Cleaning

Clear debris and buildup from gutters and downspouts to ensure proper drainage and prevent water damage to your home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my property pressure washed?

For most residential properties, we recommend pressure washing once or twice a year. Commercial properties may require more frequent cleaning depending on foot traffic and location.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

Not all surfaces can withstand the same pressure levels. Our team is trained to adjust the pressure and techniques based on the specific surface being cleaned to prevent damage.

Are your cleaning solutions environmentally friendly?

Yes, we use eco-friendly cleaning solutions that effectively remove dirt and contaminants without harming plants, animals, or water sources.

How long does a typical pressure washing service take?

Service times vary depending on the size and complexity of the job. A typical residential driveway might take 1-2 hours, while a full commercial property could take a full day or more.
