About Prestige Pressure Washing

Our Story

Prestige Pressure Washing was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional pressure washing services that restore beauty and value to properties throughout Stockton and surrounding areas.

Led by CEO Al Smith, our team of dedicated professionals brings years of experience and a commitment to quality in every job we undertake. We take pride in our attention to detail and our ability to transform dirty, stained surfaces into clean, like-new conditions.

What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted name in pressure washing services across Northern California, serving residential, commercial, and marine clients within a 75-mile radius of our Stockton headquarters.

Our Approach

Quality First

We believe in delivering the highest quality service on every project, no matter the size. Our team uses professional-grade equipment and environmentally friendly cleaning solutions to ensure optimal results without harming your property or the environment.

Customer-Focused

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We listen carefully to your needs, provide clear communication throughout the process, and ensure that the final results meet or exceed your expectations. We're not satisfied until you are.

Right Tools for the Job

Different surfaces and contaminants require different cleaning approaches. We invest in a variety of specialized equipment and techniques to ensure we're using the right tools for each specific job, delivering superior results while protecting your property.

Environmentally Responsible

We use eco-friendly cleaning solutions and water conservation techniques whenever possible, minimizing our environmental impact while still achieving excellent cleaning results.
